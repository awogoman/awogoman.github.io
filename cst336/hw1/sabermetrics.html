Modern Analytics in the Game

Sabermetrics applies computational and statistical techniques to evaluate baseball performance. Modern analysts collect millions of data points from sensors and cameras that track every pitch, swing, and fielding play. Algorithms then calculate advanced measures like WAR, wOBA, and pitch movement efficiency, revealing insights beyond traditional stats such as batting average or ERA. The result is a more objective understanding of player value and strategy, helping teams draft smarter, optimize lineups, and predict outcomes with remarkable accuracy.
