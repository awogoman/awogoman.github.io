Key Terms

Sabermetrics

Data-driven analysis of baseball statistics to evaluate player and team performance (e.g., WAR, wOBA, xFIP).

Predictive Modeling

Statistical & ML techniques (e.g., regression, time series) used to forecast performance or injury risk.

Decision Support

Tools that surface insights to coaches and analysts for scouting, lineup optimization, and in-game strategy.
