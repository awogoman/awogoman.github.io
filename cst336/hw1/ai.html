Decision Support in Baseball

Artificial intelligence extends baseball analytics into real-time decision making. AI models simulate pitch sequences, defensive alignments, and opponent tendencies to support coaches before and during games. Computer vision helps scouts evaluate players objectively through automated video analysis, and predictive models rank prospects based on future potential. These tools allow teams to make faster, data-driven choices by blending human intuition with algorithmic precision in order to gain a competitive edge on the field.
